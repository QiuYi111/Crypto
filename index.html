Research Overview

Bridging the gap between natural language understanding and financial decision-making

Problem Statement

Traditional RL trading systems rely solely on price data, missing crucial market sentiment and fundamental information embedded in news and social media.

Our Approach

We integrate LLM-extracted sentiment vectors with market data to create enhanced observations for RL agents, enabling more informed trading decisions.

Key Innovation

Novel integration of Mamba architecture for sequence modeling with multi-dimensional confidence vectors from LLM analysis.

Multi-Asset Training

Trained on BTC, ETH, SOL simultaneously to ensure generalization across different market regimes.

Real-time Sentiment

Live integration of market sentiment through LLM analysis of news and social media.

Mamba Architecture

State-of-the-art sequence modeling for capturing long-term dependencies in financial time series.

Mathematical Framework

Enhanced Observation Space:

$$ o_t = [p_t, v_t, \text{TA}_t, \text{CV}_t, \text{ID}_t] $$

Where: p = price, v = volume, TA = technical indicators, CV = confidence vector, ID = asset identifier

Reward Function:

$$ R_t = r_t - \lambda_1 \cdot \text{TC}_t - \lambda_2 \cdot \text{Risk}_t + \lambda_3 \cdot \text{Sharpe}_t $$

Balancing returns, transaction costs, risk penalties, and Sharpe ratio enhancement

Experimental Setup

Comprehensive evaluation across multiple dimensions

Dataset Details

  • Assets: BTC, ETH, SOL
  • Period: 2022-2024
  • Frequency: 1-hour intervals
  • Features: 47 dimensions total
  • Samples: ~75,000 observations

Model Architectures

  • Baseline: Standard PPO with MLP
  • Mamba-PPO: Mamba-based actor-critic
  • Mamba-SAC: Soft actor-critic variant
  • Ablation: No LLM features

Training Protocol

  • Episodes: 10,000 training episodes
  • Validation: 20% hold-out
  • Hyperparams: Bayesian optimization
  • Hardware: 8xA100 GPUs
